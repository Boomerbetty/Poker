Welcome — Become a Piranha!

Each session deals you hole cards and a seat position at a 9-player tournament table. Make the right pre-flop decision using Sklansky's 8-Group system. Every hand is scored 0–5. Your fish rank is revealed at the end.

Score 90%+ in any session to unlock 🦈 Hammerhead — the advanced difficulty tier featuring 3-bet pots, squeeze spots, and short-stack shove decisions.

  • Group 1 = Premium (AA/KK/QQ/JJ/AKs). Group 8 = Trash.
  • Position determines your playable range. BTN is widest; UTG is tightest.
  • Stack depth: 50 Big Blinds — the most critical tournament depth.
  • Five actions: Fold / Call / Raise 3BB / 3-Bet ~9BB / All-In.

How You Are Scored

Every hand is worth 5 points. Your session score is total points earned divided by the maximum possible, expressed as a percentage. Decisions are graded on a spectrum — poker mistakes are not all equal.

5/5
Perfect — Exactly the correct action for this hand, position, and scenario.
4/5
Good — A strong alternative play. Slightly sub-optimal but not a leak.
3/5
Acceptable — Directionally right but wrong sizing or aggression level.
2/5
Mistake — Wrong action but not catastrophic. A consistent leak over time.
1/5
Error — Clearly wrong. Playing trash from early position, or folding premiums.
0/5
Wrong — Worst possible choice for the spot. Costs chips every time.
